FEATURES

Everything You Need To Stay Ahead

Gain full control over your career preparation with our integrated dashboard tools designed for student success.

Interactive Dashboard

Get a bird's-eye view of your recruitment process. Monitor active job applications, selected status, current coding streaks, and recent application listings in real-time.

Application Pipeline

Track and organize your job applications end-to-end. Keep records of company names, roles, packages, dates, and interview stages with clean status filters.

DSA Tracker

Log solved coding questions grouped by data structure topics. Keep tabs on total solved counts, completion percentages, and manage your progress across structures.

Secure Profile Manager

Manage your academic metadata, resumes, location details, and profile pictures. Keep contact credentials and authentication tokens securely saved.

OUR MISSION

Bridging the Gap Between Preparation & Placement

Placement Tracker was built with a simple goal: to help students navigate the chaotic placement season with absolute structure and confidence.

By combining daily DSA consistency metrics with an organized job application pipeline, the platform ensures you never miss a deadline and always stay motivated.

✓ Consistency-First: Built-in streaks to cultivate daily coding habits.
✓ Pipeline Transparency: Track OAs, interviews, and offers in one spot.
✓ Progress Tracking: Stay on top of recent applications, streaks, and interview stages.
